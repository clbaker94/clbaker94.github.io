About Us

Annyeong is a Texas based company that was created in 2018. The founder of Annyeong wanted to provide beginning level Korean language learners with a fun and easy way to begin their studies. The modules will cover a range of topics from greetings to food & restaurants. So if you would like to begin learning Korean, Annyeong is the perfect application for you!

About the Founder

Hello everyone, my name is Courtney Baker! I began studying the basics of Korean on my own in 2010 and I started taking Korean language classes at my university in 2013. I had the opportunity to study abroad twice in Seoul in 2014 and 2016. I feel that my Korean language skills improved immensely during my time abroad because I was constantly exposed to the language. This allowed me to gain better listening comprehension and speaking skills.

However, I understand that everyone may not have the ability to immerse themselves in the culture by studying abroad or traveling to Korea. So, I wanted to create this application to help those of you who would like to begin learning Korean by providing you with modules that focus on listening comprehension and pronunciation. I wish you all the best of luck in your Korean language studies and hope that you enjoy the lessons that Annyeong has to offer!
