Annyeong!

Welcome to Annyeong! By using our modules you can learn basic Korean vocabulary words and improve your pronunciation. Each module will contain a list of vocabulary words covering the specified topic. Clicking on the word will allow you to hear an audio sample of the vocabulary word. Along with the audio samples, the written Korean translation and Romanized version of the word will also be provided. Clicking on the information icon in the modules will provide you with more details about the vocabulary word. Click on the Modules tab below to begin your lessons!

If you would like to tell us about your experience using Annyeong or provide us with suggestions for improvement, we would love to hear your feedback! You can do this by clicking on the Feedback tab and filling out and submitting the form.
