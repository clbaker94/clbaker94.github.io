Numbers

This module will focus on the numbers 1-10 in both native and Sino-Korean (of Chinese origin) numbers. Native Korean numbers are most commonly used for counting or with counter words (only for numbers 1-99). They are also used when stating your age. Sino-Korean numbers are used when discussing dates, money, time (excluding the hour), number sequences (i.e. phone numbers) , and numbers over 100.
